# Hub Educação Sem Muros (Education Without Borders Hub) 📚🌐

> **University Extension Project**
> Developed by **Wellington Reis da Silva Filho** as part of the *Systems Analysis and Development (ADS)* degree at Cruzeiro do Sul University / UNICID.

The **Hub Educação Sem Muros** was born from the conviction that, in a world saturated with information, the true barrier to learning is not the lack of content, but the difficulty in finding reliable and structured sources.

This website acts as a strategic digital repository, functioning as a specialized curated collection that connects students to a vast network of free knowledge. Our platform is a social intervention tool that leverages technology to democratize learning, undergoing continuous updates to ensure that every link adds genuine academic and professional value.

## 💻 Technology and UX Design

While built on a modern architecture, the interface was engineered to be highly intuitive and responsive. We applied **UX Design** concepts to ensure seamless navigation, removing invisible barriers between citizens and information.

### Tech Stack:
* **HTML5** – Semantic structuring of the digital repository.
* **CSS3** – Responsive design and modern visual styling.
* **JavaScript** – Interactivity and dynamic link filtering.
